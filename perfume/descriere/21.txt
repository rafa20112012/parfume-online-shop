In 1989, a team of dermatologists and pharmacists set up the cosmetic brand Sesderma. Based on direct contact with patients, they have developed specific products for various skin problems. Sesderma was the first pharmaceutical company in Spain to develop glycolic acid creams.
Thanks to the rich experience gained by Sesderma through customer feedback, it now offers a wide range of products, from several cosmetic product lines. The company constantly conducts additional research and uses technological advancement to provide customers with the best products for their needs.
Their high-quality cosmetics are now sold in more than 40 countries, and interest in them is growing. Sesderma has received a number of prestigious awards. Try the special products from the Sesderma brand, which will understand your requirements!
