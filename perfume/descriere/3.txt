The composition of the perfume
Lacoste Pour Femme starts with a sweet and slightly spicy note of allspice, complemented by the delicate freesia accordion. The floral heart intoxicates you with essences of jasmine, heliotrope, hibiscus and roses. The base contains cedar and sandalwood accents, incense and warm musk.
